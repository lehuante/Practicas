Open Water Diver

A highly-trained PADI Instructor will teach you to scuba dive in a relaxed, supportive learning environment. You’ll complete your PADI eLearning, shallow water practice dives (in a pool or pool-like environment) and open water training dives (in the ocean, lake or quarry).

By the end of the course, you will have the skills and knowledge to dive at home or abroad with another certified diver.
